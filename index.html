Mazowian Dark & Grey

A Small Dog Breed from the Mazowian Region of Poland

About the Breed

Mazowian Dark & Grey is a small dog breed originating from the Mazowian region of Poland. The breed was first recognized by major kennel clubs in 2024.

Appearance

Mazowian Dark & Grey dogs are small, weighing between 2 to 5 kilograms, with long hair. Their coat comes in black, white, and grey colors.

Temperament

These dogs are known for their friendly and playful nature. They are highly emotionally intelligent, and they love greeting their owners and playing with other dogs. Mazowian Dark & Grey dogs are great with children and other pets.

Health and Grooming

Mazowian Dark & Grey dogs have no specific health concerns. They enjoy daily walks, preferably two or more times a day. Their coat can be left to grow out or cut short, depending on the owner's preference.

Energy and Exercise

These dogs have mid to high energy levels, but they also spend a significant amount of time resting. They require at least an hour of exercise per day.

Breed History

The breed's history and its role in the development of other breeds remain a mystery.

In Popular Culture

The character Westunczyk from the line of kids toys created by the Rainbow Dog Toy Company is an example of a Mazowian Dark & Grey dog.

Working Roles

Mazowian Dark & Grey dogs are primarily companion animals.

Misconceptions

Some people mistakenly believe that Mazowian Dark & Grey dogs bark excessively, but this is not true. They do, however, have distinct personalities.

Housing

This breed is well-suited for indoor living and does not require a yard.

Tips for First-Time Owners

New owners should feed their Mazowian Dark & Grey dogs high-quality cooked food, avoiding raw food.

Breeder Selection

When selecting a breeder for a Mazowian Dark & Grey dog, ensure they are licensed.
